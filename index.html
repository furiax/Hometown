Top three activities in Hamme

Walk in nature

Hamme is surrounded by the rivers Schelde and Durme, making it an excellent place for walks along the dikes.

De Grote Napoleon

The last remaining windmill in Hamme. Today, it has a bakery and tearoom next to it.

De Hamse Wuitens

A folklore group that takes to the streets every year during Carnival.
